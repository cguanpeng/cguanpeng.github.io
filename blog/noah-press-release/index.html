Noah’s Dilemma – A Sudoku-Inspired Strategy Roguelike Sets Sail with First Trailer & Demo

Feb 10, 2025

For Immediate Release

I’m excited to share the first public trailer and demo for Noah’s Dilemma, my upcoming sudoku-inspired strategy roguelike. In this game, you’ll assemble a crew of animal sailors, strategically position them to survive the ever-changing seas, and overcome unpredictable challenges on your journey through a reimagined Noah’s Ark mythos.

After originally prototyping the game in 2021, I returned to it in late 2023 as a full-time indie developer, refining its mechanics into a unique fusion of tactical placement and roguelike adventure. Now, it’s finally time to share the first glimpse of what’s to come.

About the Game

Noah’s Dilemma blends puzzle-like strategy with roguelike unpredictability. Every decision—where to place your crew, which islands to explore, how to prepare for the journey ahead—can mean the difference between survival and disaster. Discover powerful synergies, adapt to evolving challenges, and navigate a perilous world where each run tells a different story.

About the Developer

I’m a solo indie game developer currently based in Fujisawa, Japan. My previous game, Booth: A Dystopian Adventure, was released in 2020. Noah’s Dilemma marks my first fully solo project, where I even took on composing the soundtrack myself.

If you’d like to cover Noah’s Dilemma, I’d be happy to provide more details, assets, or an interview. Let me know if you’re interested!
